/obj/structure/overmap/level

Z-level linked overmap object

These are exactly what they say on the tin, overmap objects that are linked to one or more z-levels. There is nothing special on this side, but overmap ships treat them differently from all other overmap objects, such as the fact they can dock on said z-level.
